About Me

Hi! My name is Daniel Vo and I am a Computational and Systems Biology major at the University of California, Los Angeles. My hobbies include running, playing music and League of Legends.

Coding has been a relatively new interest for me, having started learning HTML, CSS and JavaScript from freeCodeCamp.org at the beginning 2018. I came across coding in one of my life science classes which used Python for modeling population growth, and have since been exploring. I am currently taking an introductory course on C++ at UCLA to improve my fundamentals.
